# Privacy Policy for Lockscreen Todo

**Last Updated: February 26, 2026**

## Introduction

Lockscreen Todo is a simple tool that generates custom lockscreen wallpapers with tasks, reminders, and photos. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our service.

By using Lockscreen Todo, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1. Account Information
When you create an account via Clerk, we collect:
- **Email address** - used for account identification and communication
- **User ID** - unique identifier for your account
- **Name (optional)** - if provided during sign-up

### 1.2. Content Data
When you generate wallpapers, we process:
- **Task text** - your to-do items and reminders
- **Uploaded photos** - images you choose for backgrounds
- **Generated wallpapers** - the final wallpaper images you create

### 1.3. Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- **Log files** - IP address, browser type, referring pages
- **Device information** - device type, operating system
- **Usage patterns** - how you interact with our service (for improvement purposes)

### 1.4. Payment Information
When you purchase premium features via Lemon Squeezy:
- **Payment details** - processed securely by Lemon Squeezy
- **Transaction records** - subscription status and payment history

**Important**: We never store your credit card information. All payments are handled securely by Lemon Squeezy.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

- **Service delivery** - to generate wallpapers and manage your account
- **Improvement** - to analyze usage patterns and enhance our service
- **Communication** - to send updates about new features (only with your consent)
- **Support** - to assist you when you need help
- **Security** - to prevent fraud and protect your account

## 3. Data Storage and Retention

### 3.1. Where Your Data is Stored
- **Account data**: Stored in Supabase (encrypted database)
- **Generated wallpapers**: Stored temporarily in browser storage or Supabase
- **Session data**: Managed by Clerk (secure authentication)

### 3.2. Data Retention
- **Active accounts**: Your data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: Your data is permanently deleted within 30 days
- **Temporary data**: Generated wallpapers may be automatically deleted after 7 days

## 5. Notion Integration Privacy

We understand that connecting your Notion account involves trusting us with your task data. Here's how we protect it:

### 5.1. What We Access
- ✅ **Read access** to your Notion task databases
- ✅ **Task titles and properties** (text, due dates, status)
- ✅ **Database structure** (to identify task databases)

### 5.2. What We Do NOT Access
- ❌ **Other databases** - We only access databases with "task", "todo", "待办", or "任务" in the name
- ❌ **Write permissions** - We never create, modify, or delete anything in your Notion
- ❌ **Personal notes or documents** - Only tasks are accessed
- ❌ **Private settings** - Your Notion settings remain private

### 5.3. Data Usage
- **Only for**: Generating your personal lockscreen wallpapers
- **Never for**: Analysis, AI training, or sharing with third parties

### 5.4. You Control Access
- **Revoke anytime**: You can disconnect Notion in your Notion settings
- **Selective**: Only import tasks when you click the "Import" button
- **Secure**: Your Notion access token is encrypted and stored separately
