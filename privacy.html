Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 7, 2026

1. Overview

Price to Hours is a Chrome extension that converts prices into hours of work. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

2. Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • ❌ No personal information
  • ❌ No browsing history
  • ❌ No search queries
  • ❌ No purchase data
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No device identifiers
  • ❌ No cookies or tracking

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your hourly rate settings
  • Your income configuration
  • Extension preferences (enabled/disabled)

This data is stored using Chrome Storage API and never leaves your device.

4. Permissions

Price to Hours requests the following permissions:

Permission Why We Need It
storage Save your settings locally
tabs Detect when you navigate to new pages
<all_urls> Read price text on any webpage

5. Third-Party Services

We do NOT use:

  • ❌ No analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • ❌ No advertising networks
  • ❌ No tracking pixels
  • ❌ No external servers
  • ❌ No cloud sync

The extension works entirely offline after installation.

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT share:

  • ❌ No data sold to third parties
  • ❌ No data shared with advertisers
  • ❌ No data shared with data brokers
  • ❌ No data shared with government agencies

Simply put: We have no data to share.

7. Children's Privacy

Price to Hours does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data at all, this is straightforward.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new "Last Updated" date.
