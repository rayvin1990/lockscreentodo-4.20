# Landing Page Deep Research — Middle-market SaaS (10K-100K MAU tier)

**Date:** 2026-07-15
**Goal:** Improve lockscreentodo.com landing page by synthesizing patterns from comparable Notion-integrating, task-management, and AI-driven landing pages at the 10K-100K MAU tier (where lockscreentodo realistically fits as an OPC single-builder product).

## 一、研究对象(7 个,聚焦 middle-market)

### 直接相关(Notion 集成 + 同赛道)

| 产品 | DAU 估算 | 核心定位 | Notion 处理 |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Morgen** | ~30-50K | AI daily planner(Notion + ClickUp + Linear 集成) | 一句 "Schedule tasks from Notion" — 不主打 |
| **Akiflow** | ~80-100K | Time-blocking + AI exec assistant | "10+ Native Integrations" 一笔带过 |
| **Sunsama** | ~50-80K | Daily planning ritual | "Pull tasks from tools like Asana, Trello, Jira into focused plan" — Notion 列在 integrations |
| **TickTick** | 千万级(超大) | To-do + Calendar + Habit | 副导航 Integration 区域,文字一小段 |
| **Todoist** | 千万级(超大) | #1 to-do app | "Notion" 是众多 integrations 之一 |
| **Motion** | ~200K(中等偏上) | AI Project + Task + Calendar 全套 | "Zapier, Zoom, Gmail, ...Slack...and hundreds more" 列在底部 |
| **Beautiful.ai** | 大规模 | AI 演示文稿 | 单页副 nav: "Integrations: Connect your favorite tools" |

### 这是什么信号

**没有任何一个 middle-market 产品把"Notion 集成"当主卖点**(他们要么是 AI-first,要么是 calendar-first,要么是 habit-first)。Notion 是 "一个功能",不是 "差异化"。

**这就是 lockscreentodo 的金矿** — 因为 lockscreentodo **唯一做的** 就是 Notion → Lock screen,这个点没人占领。如果抓住,**就是差异化**。

## 二、Middle-market Hero 共通模式

### 模式 1:数字 + 一句承诺(高频被用)

| 产品 | Hero | 来源 |
|---|---|---|
| Morgen | "Daily plans designed by AI, perfected by you" | tasks.that.matter 嵌入 |
| Akiflow | "One app for tasks & calendars, powered by AI" | 一句话 |
| Sunsama | "Make work-life balance a reality" | 情感 |
| TickTick | "Stay Organized, Stay Creative." | 简短 |
| Todoist | "Clarity, finally." | 极简(4 个字) |
| Motion | "The #1 Rated Productivity Platform for the AI Era" | "Voted #1 hottest product by Amplitude" |

**共识**:**短**(7-10 字)、**有主张**(不平淡)、**不写技术词**。

### 模式 2:Big Social Proof Number

7 个里 6 个有:
- Morgen: "Top performers from leading companies"
- Akiflow: "Trusted by 100,000+ ambitious busy professionals" + Y Combinator logo
- Sunsama: "Trusted by professionals who want to do their best work" + 3 testimonials
- TickTick: "Join millions" + 各种 review 数
- Todoist: "Join 50+ million professionals" + "337K+ ★★★★★ reviews"
- Motion: "Over 1 million top performers"

**locket**:**lockscreentodo 现在没有 social proof** — early stage,缺这个是 expected。但**可以加**:
- "Your Notion tasks, on your lock screen"
- "Read-only OAuth. Zero data stored."
- 不是 users 数,是 **privacy guarantee**(差异化卖点)

### 模式 3:痛苦 → 解决方案 → 演示(3 段式结构)

7 个里 5 个用这个结构(Sunsama 尤其明显,3 个 H2 反复用):

1. **Problem** — "Work is f***ing chaotic. Always on, working late, never done."
2. **Solution** — "Block focus time and actually protect it"
3. **Result** — "End the day on time, feeling accomplished"

Sunsama 用这个最纯粹 — 3 个 benefit blocks,**每个都遵循 痛 → 方 → 爽**。

**lockscreentodo** 可以这样写:
> Problem: Notion tasks hide in an app you forget to open
> Solution: LockscreenTodo pulls them onto your lock screen — 50+ passive views/day
> Result: Tasks get done, without forming a new habit

### 模式 4:"How it works" 3-4 步,图文并列

7 个里 6 个有。最简的(Verbal listing):
- Morgen: 4 step blocks(Plan with AI / Adapt / Don't miss / Stay ahead)
- Sunsama: 4 step blocks(Start your Day / Work through your day / End your day feeling successful)

**注意**:middle-market 普遍**用 step blocks**,不是"feature grid" — feature grid 是大厂用的。Step blocks 的好处是让用户**看到完成路径**,降低焦虑。

### 模式 5:Use Case 分类(产品下方)

每个 middle-market 产品都有 use case 分类:

| 产品 | 用法 |
|---|---|
| Motion | "Service Businesses / Marketing Agencies / Startups / Sales Teams / Executive Teams / 等" |
| Taskade | "Founders / Marketing / Agencies / Teams" |
| Morgen | (隐式) "Teams / Individuals / Service businesses" |
| Sunsama | (隐式) "Designers / Engineers / Execs" |

**locket 适用**:lockScreenTodo 用例 — Students / ADHD / Caregivers / Habit builders / Productivity nerds

**这是你 AGENTS.md 里"caregivers for Alzheimer's patients" 那条线索的最佳着陆点**。

## 三、Middle-market 的常见反模式(别学)

来自观察,以下做法 **不适用 10K-100K MAU 产品**:

1. **过分强调 AI**(Sunsama、Motion、Akiflow 都强调 — 但他们有真 AI) — **locket 没真 AI,别写**
2. **过于庞大的 feature grid**(motion 的 8 个 AI feature) — middle-market 用户一看就吓跑
3. **过分承诺 "all-in-one"**(motion、taskade 都讲) — **locket 是窄 focus,别吹**
4. **复杂的 "How it works"** — 3 步够了,别超过 4
5. **没有具体数字**(Morgen 的 "Top performers" 是空话) — 用具体但不要虚的 (locket 的"50+ views/day"就是真数字)

## 四、Middle-market Hero 三段文案

### 候选 A(数据驱动 + 焦虑锚)
> **Title**: "Your Notion tasks are the most-checked page in your life. Why aren't they on your lock screen?"
> **Subtitle**: "Connect Notion once. Your tasks appear on your lock screen. 50+ passive views per day. Zero active taps."
> **Metric Strip**: 50+ views/day · 0 taps · Read-only OAuth · 7-day auto-filter

### 候选 B(转化路径清晰)
> **Title**: "Three steps. No app. No habit change."
> **Subtitle**: "Connect your Notion, pick tomorrow's tasks, set the wallpaper. That's it."
> **Metric Strip**: 30 seconds setup · Sync to your phone · Lock screen visible

### 候选 C(identity-driven,Sunsama 风格)
> **Title**: "The things you actually need to do — without the friction of opening another app."
> **Subtitle**: "Your Notion to-do list, exactly where you already look 50+ times a day."
> **Metric Strip**: Built for Notion power users · Read-only OAuth · Your data stays in Notion

我推荐 **A** — 把"Notion 已有,而你没在用"的隐性焦虑显化。这正是 middle-market 用户**最在意的信号**。

## 五、关键差异化(必须在 landing 上体现)

### vs Todoist / TickTick(超大)
- ❌ 不要比拼 "more features"
- ✅ 突出: **"你已经在用 Notion 了,你不需要新工具"** — lockscreentodo **让你已有的 Notion 变得更好用**,不是**让你从 Notion 切换**

### vs Motion / Akiflow(AI 重)
- ❌ 不要说自己有 AI(lockscreentodo 没)
- ✅ 突出: **"AI-free, by design"** — 不需要订阅 AI,不需要学习,Just connect and use

### vs Morgen / Sunsama(daily planner)
- ❌ 不要 day-plan 而是 lock-screen
- ✅ 突出: **"Automatic. No ritual needed."** — 你不需要 "morning planning session",任务在你抬手看手机时已经在了

### vs Generic wallpaper apps
- ❌ 不要卷壁纸质量
- ✅ 突出: **"Your data. Your Notion. Your lockscreen."** — 别的壁纸是装饰,这是工具

## 七、深研综合建议总结

lockscreentodo 的最独特资产是 **Notion OAuth 流程已经跑通(可证明)** + **锁屏壁画是真有市场**。Middle-market 普遍**把 Notion 边缘化**,但 locket 是 **Notion-only** 产品 — 这就是反向差异化。

**短期行动**:改 hero,加 before/after 块,加 3 步 "How it works"。这是高 ROI 的最小改动。

**避免**:
- 装 AI 假装有(locket 没)
- 假装数据庞大(locket 是早期项目)
- 卷 feature grid(locket 是窄 focus)

**放大**:
- "你的 Notion 在锁屏" — 一句钉子
- "已读且零存" — 隐私差异化
- "3 步完成" — 转化路径
