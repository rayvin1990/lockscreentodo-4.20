# User Guide

## Quick Settings

**Default settings are optimized. Install and use immediately.**

---

### Want to Customize? (30 seconds)

**1. Click the Extension Icon** ⏱️

Located in Chrome toolbar (top right).

---

**2. Enter Your Income**

Example: `3000`

---

**3. Select Pay Period**

| Type | For |
|------|-----|
| **Hourly** | Part-time, hourly workers |
| **Daily** | Freelancers, contractors |
| **Weekly** | Weekly payroll |
| **Biweekly** ← Recommended | US standard payroll |
| **Monthly** | Monthly salary |
| **Yearly** | Annual salary |

---

**4. Set Hours per Day**

Default: `8` hours

Common settings:
- Standard full-time: 8 hours
- 996 schedule: 9-10 hours
- Part-time: 4-6 hours
- Freelance: Custom

---

**5. Click Save Settings**

Done.

---

## Example Configurations

### Example 1: US Full-Time Employee
```
Income: 3000
Type: Biweekly Pay
Hours/Day: 8
→ Hourly rate ≈ $18.75/hour
```

### Example 2: China Monthly Salary

Income: 15000

## How It Works

**Automatic Conversion:**
- All pay types → converted to hourly rate
- Based on your hours/day setting
- Auto-considers work days (Biweekly=10, Monthly=21.75, Yearly=260)

**Time Unit Display:**
| Hours Worked | Display Unit |
|--------------|--------------|
| < 12 hours | `X hours` |
| 12-40 hours | `X days` |
| 40-174 hours | `X weeks` |
| 174-500 hours | `X months` |
| 500+ hours | `X years` |

---

## Enable/Disable

**Temporarily disable:**
1. Click extension icon
2. Toggle **Enable Extension** switch
3. Tooltips will stop appearing

**Re-enable:**
- Toggle the switch again
